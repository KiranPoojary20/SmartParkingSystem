Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find available parking spots?
Open the app and view real-time parking availability in your selected area. Green markers indicate free spots, red indicates occupied spaces.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit cards, mobile wallets, and in-app payment methods. Prepaid parking credits are also available.
How accurate is spot availability?
Our system uses real-time sensors and machine learning to provide 99.8% accurate parking spot information.
